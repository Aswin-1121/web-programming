KERALA

wikipedia says:

Kerala (English: /ˈkɛrələ/ Malayalam: [ke:ɾɐɭɐm] is a state on the southwestern Malabar Coast of India. It was formed on 1 November 1956, following the passage of the States Reorganisation Act, by combining Malayalam-speaking regions of the erstwhile states of Travancore-Cochin and Madras. Spread over 38,863 km2 (15,005 sq mi), Kerala is the twenty-first largest Indian state by area.
